Cohort retention builder

Paste the retention query's CSV output. It rolls weekly go-live cohorts into monthly lines, splits May into early and late, and omits October. Nothing leaves your browser.

Expected columns

Header must include Cohort (ISO week like 2025-W45 or 202545) and week columns W1…W7. A go-lives count column is optional but required for the weighting option. Blank cells are fine and stay unplotted.
